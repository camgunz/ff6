So weapons have 4 extra bytes for the elemental absorb/null/weak and the 2nd
condition effect, and other equipment has 2 extra bytes for weapon props and
special attack/evade.

There are only 4 field effect flags, so conceivably they could go in the first
half of byte 28 for defensive equipment.
